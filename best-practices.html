Best practices

Simple habits that strengthen your defenses

Strong passwords

Create long, unique passwords with a mix of words, symbols, and numbers. Use a password manager when possible.

Multi-factor authentication

Enable MFA wherever possible so a stolen password is not enough to access your account.

Software updates

Install updates promptly to patch known vulnerabilities in your operating system and apps.

Safe browsing

Verify URLs, avoid suspicious downloads, and be cautious with unexpected attachments.

Public Wi-Fi precautions

Use a VPN and avoid sensitive tasks when connected to public or shared networks.

Backup strategy

Keep regular backups of important files in at least two locations, including offline storage.
